How We Teach You

All of our music classes use specialized teaching techniques, including private and group rehearsals. Rock Academy guides our students through a guarenteed process to eventually perform in front of a live audience.

Drum Lessons

Rock Academy's music lessons help our students quickly learn their favorite songs with the goal of performing in front of a crowd. Drum students are able to learn at their own pace, and will be given the tools to dissect even the most difficult patterns.
